Doritos Cereal

Description:

Crushing up Doritos never sounds like a good idea but pouring Mountain Dew on top of that is pure blasphemy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of your favourite doritos
  • 1 canned soft drink of your choice(I prefer Mountain Dew)

Steps:

  1. take an empty bowl
  2. crush the doritos packet like your life depends on it!
  3. Now open the packet and pour the crushed doritos into your bowl
  4. This is the best part, Open your canned soft drink and slowly pour it over crushed doritos with pleasure - just like how you unbox your new iphone
  5. Now take out your spoon or fork(I prefer my bare hands) and enjoy your doritos cereal!!!
